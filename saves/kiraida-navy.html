Mitsubishi Type-120 "Sea Tengu"

The Sea Tengu was considered as the most iconic naval-based fighter jets known in South Karemi, seen service in Kiraidan Royal Naval Air Force (KRNA), and possibly outside of New Kiraida.

First saw service on board of their first carrier, KRNS White Dragon of the 12th Kido-Butai Fleet in 1974, the Mitsubishi Type-120 "Sea Tengu" naval fighter finally saw wide spread service in other fleet, most prominently by the 1st Kido-Butai Fleet in 1979, and the 88th Deep Blue Fleet in 1982.

Like every early models of military hardware, Sea Tengu isn't without issues. The Mitsubishi Type-120 Sea Tengu was actually designed to be a stand-in supliment for the Nakajima Ki-111 "Blue Falcon" Attack aircraft, which ended up too overweight after the Kiraidan Royal Army Air Force (KRAAF) had stepped in to utilize the existing project.

And for cost-saving measures, Type-120 Sea Tengu had used the same engine also used by the grossly overweight Nakajima Ki-111 Blue Falcon, which is developed by Setsu Electrics.

The dawn of a new age. For the Kiraidan Royal Navy.

First ordered by the previous king of Kiraida in 1904, she was considered as the most modern pieces of military hardware to ever be built for the Kiraidan Royal Navy.

Built to Kennedy's original specification of her to be armed with ten 12"/45cal guns in five two-gunned turrets, she was one to not be underestimated. Unlike most other dreadnoughts that came before her, she wasn't built with superfiring turrets, mainly out of grounded concern that superfiring turrets might raise her center of gravity a tad too high, making her prone to heeling too much during the turn. During her construction, she was christened as Kamishirasawa Class Dreadnought.

She isn't the only one to be constructed as she had three other sisterships built in other three major shipyards, the Horatio Alger Naval Arsenals, Fubuki Naval Arsenals, and Ticonderoga Naval Shipyard

The Old Reliable

The Gale Class Destroyers are a rather unique class of destroyer to be built in the Kiraidan Navy, at least before the Worthless War between Rothania and Kiraida. Back then, only seven of these ships were built with the goal in mind to make them as cheap as possible, removing anything ornamental on it such as decorative crests, flashy paint scheme, and over-the-top hull design, opting for more bare-minimum hull design.

She is fairly standard for her time, being armed with four 120mm guns in four singles. Ever since the rise of flying menace all around the world, she was given five 7.7mm machine guns to at least deter any pestering aircraft from approaching her.

Her torpedo armament was fairly standard for her time, being two triple 533mm torpedo launchers, facing in opposite directions.

Due to her nation of origin have more oil than coal, she was, naturally, an oil-burner instead of a coal-burner, giving her higher performance than any coal-fired or coal-oil-mix fired destroyers, and thus giving her maximum range of 4,800 nautical miles
